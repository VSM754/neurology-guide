MBBS to Neurology in the USA

A step‑by‑step roadmap for international medical graduates.

Overview

This guide explains how MBBS graduates can become neurologists in the U.S. through USMLE exams, residency, and fellowship.

Step 1: Complete MBBS

  • Finish MBBS (5.5 years)
  • Maintain strong academics
  • Gain clinical exposure

Step 2: USMLE Exams

  • Step 1: Basic sciences
  • Step 2 CK: Clinical knowledge
  • OET: English proficiency

Step 3: ECFMG Certification

  • Pass USMLE exams
  • Verify degree

Step 4: Build Profile

  • U.S. clinical experience
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Research

Step 5: Residency

  • 1 year Internal Medicine
  • 3 years Neurology

Step 6: Visa

  • J‑1 (common)
  • H‑1B (limited)

Step 7: Fellowship (Optional)

  • Stroke, Epilepsy, Neurocritical Care, etc.

Step 8: Board Certification

  • Pass ABPN exam
  • Start practice

Timeline

Stage Duration
MBBS 5.5 years
USMLE + ECFMG 1–2 years
Residency 4 years
Fellowship 1–3 years
Total 11–14 years

Key Takeaways

  • Strong USMLE scores are critical
  • U.S. experience improves chances
  • Neurology is achievable for IMGs
