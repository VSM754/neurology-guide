Green Card vs No Green Card: Education Impact

How immigration status shapes tuition, financial aid, and career pathways.

Executive Summary

Immigration status significantly impacts tuition and medical career access.

  • Same opportunities

College

Green Card

  • More aid

  • Higher tuition
  • Limited aid
