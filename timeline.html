Timeline: 8th Grade to Neurologist

Simple roadmap from middle school to a neurology career.

Overview

This timeline shows the full journey from 8th grade to becoming a neurologist.

8th Grade

  • Focus on science & math basics
  • Build study habits
  • Join clubs

9th–10th Grade

  • Biology, Chemistry
  • Science competitions
  • Early volunteering

11th–12th Grade

  • AP courses
  • SAT/ACT
  • Shadow doctors

After High School

  • USA: Pre-med → MD → Residency
  • India: NEET → MBBS → MD → DM
