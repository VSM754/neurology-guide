High School Plan for a Future Neurologist

Parent-friendly tone: This section gives structured guidance for each grade.

9th Grade

  • Focus on Biology, Algebra, and English.
  • Join science clubs or STEM programs.
  • Start light volunteering (library, community events).

10th Grade

  • Take advanced science courses.
  • Participate in science fairs.
  • Explore neuroscience videos and books.

11th Grade

  • AP Biology, AP Chemistry recommended.
  • Prepare for SAT/ACT.
  • Shadow a doctor if possible.

12th Grade

  • Finalize college applications.
  • Continue volunteering.
  • Maintain strong grades.

Immigration Impact on Education

Parent-friendly tone: Clear explanations of how status affects opportunities.

Green Card vs No Green Card

  • Green card = in-state tuition + FAFSA + all medical schools open.
  • No green card = international tuition + limited medical schools.

Age-Out Rule

Children on H-4 must switch to F-1 at age 21.
