NEET Registration from the United States

Step‑by‑step guide for U.S.-based students.

Overview

NEET is required for MBBS and medical programs. Registration is fully online, but you must travel to India for the exam.

Step 1: Check Eligibility

  • 12th grade with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • India/OCI/NRI eligibility

Step 2: Visit Official Website

  • Check dates and bulletin

Step 3: Documents

  • Photo & signature
  • Passport
  • School details

Step 4: Register

  • Create login
  • Save Application Number

Step 5: Fill Form

  • Personal + academic details
  • Upload documents

Step 6: Pay Fee

  • Card / UPI / Net banking

Step 7: Confirmation

  • Download confirmation page

Step 8: Correction Window

  • Edit details if needed

Step 9: Admit Card

  • Download before exam

Step 10: Travel to India

  • Arrive early
  • Carry documents

Step 11: After Exam

  • Check results
  • Attend counseling
