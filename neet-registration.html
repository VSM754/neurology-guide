How to Register for NEET from the United States

A complete, student‑friendly guide for U.S.‑based applicants.

Overview

This guide provides a simple, step‑by‑step process for students living in the United States who want to register for NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test). NEET is mandatory for MBBS, BDS, and most medical programs in India and abroad. Registration is fully online, but the student must be in India on the exam day.

Step 1: Check NEET Eligibility

  • Must have completed or be appearing for 12th grade (or equivalent).
  • Required subjects:
    • Biology/Biotechnology
    • Physics
    • Chemistry
  • Must be an Indian citizen, OCI, NRI, or foreign national.
  • U.S. 12th grade is accepted as equivalent.

Step 2: Visit the Official NEET Website

  • Go to the National Testing Agency (NTA) NEET portal.
  • Download the Information Bulletin.
  • Note:
    • Application start and end dates.
    • Correction window.
    • Exam date.

Step 3: Gather Required Documents

Prepare the following before starting:

  • Passport‑size photo (recent).
  • Signature (scanned).
  • Left‑hand thumb impression (if required).
  • Passport (recommended for NRI/OCI students).
  • U.S. school details (name, address, subjects).
  • Parent details (name, occupation, phone, email).
  • Payment method (international card or Indian card/UPI).

Step 4: Register Online

  1. Click New Registration on the NEET website.
  2. Enter:
    • Name
    • Date of birth
    • Mobile number
    • Email ID
  3. Create a password and note the Application Number.

Step 5: Fill the Application Form

  • Enter personal details.
  • Enter communication address (U.S. + India if applicable).
  • Enter academic details (10th & 12th equivalent).
  • Select exam city preferences.
  • Upload:
    • Photo
    • Signature
    • Other required documents

Step 6: Pay the NEET Application Fee

  • Pay online using:
    • Credit/debit card
    • Net banking
    • UPI
  • Save the payment confirmation.

Step 7: Download the Confirmation Page

  • After submission, download the confirmation page.
  • Save it as a PDF for future use.

Step 8: Correction Window

  • Log in using Application Number and password.
  • Edit allowed fields.
  • Upload corrected documents if needed.

Step 9: Download the Admit Card

  • Released a few weeks before the exam.
  • Enter Application Number and date of birth.
  • Print multiple copies.

Step 10: Travel to India for the Exam

  • Arrive 7–10 days before the exam.
  • Carry:
    • Admit card
    • Passport/Aadhaar
    • Additional documents listed on the admit card
  • Reach the center early.

Step 11: After the Exam

  • Check results on the NTA website.
  • Download the Scorecard.
  • Participate in counseling (state or national) depending on category.

Summary Checklist

  • Check eligibility.
  • Download NEET bulletin.
  • Gather documents.
  • Register online.
  • Fill application.
  • Pay fee.
  • Download confirmation.
  • Use correction window.
  • Download admit card.
  • Travel to India.
  • Write exam.
  • Attend counseling.
