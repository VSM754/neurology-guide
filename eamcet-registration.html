How to Register for EAMCET from the United States

A step‑by‑step guide for U.S.‑based students.

Overview

This guide explains how students living in the United States can register for AP EAMCET (EAPCET) entirely online and prepare for the exam in India.

Step 1: Identify the Correct Exam

  • AP EAMCET / EAPCET
  • Choose stream: Engineering (E), Agriculture & Pharmacy (AM), or both

Step 2: Check Important Dates

  • Application start & end dates
  • Correction window
  • Exam schedule

Step 3: Gather Documents

  • Personal and education details
  • U.S. school information
  • Passport / Aadhaar
  • Photo & signature
  • Payment method

Step 4: Pay Fee

  • Use card, net banking, or UPI
  • Save Payment ID

Step 5: Fill Application

  • Enter details and upload documents
  • Select exam centers
  • Save Application Number

Step 6: Correction Window

  • Edit details if needed

Step 7: Hall Ticket

  • Download before exam
  • Print copies

Step 8: Travel to India

  • Arrive 5–7 days early
  • Carry required documents

Step 9: After Exam

  • Check results
  • Attend counseling

Checklist

  • Apply
  • Pay fee
  • Download hall ticket
  • Attend exam
  • Complete counseling
