How to Register for EAMCET from the United States

A simple, step‑by‑step guide for U.S.‑based students applying to AP EAMCET (EAPCET).

Overview

This guide provides a clean, step‑by‑step process for students living in the United States who want to register for AP EAMCET (also called EAPCET). The entire application can be completed online, and the student only needs to be physically present in India on the exam day.

Step 1: Identify the Correct Exam

  • AP EAMCET / EAPCET (Engineering, Agriculture & Pharmacy).
  • Choose your stream:
    • Engineering (E)
    • Agriculture & Pharmacy (AM)
    • Or both, if needed.

Step 2: Check Notification & Important Dates

  • Visit the official AP EAPCET website for the current year.
  • Download the Information Brochure.
  • Note:
    • Application start date.
    • Application end date.
    • Correction window.
    • Exam dates.

Step 3: Gather Required Documents

Prepare the following before starting the application:

  • Personal details (name, date of birth, gender, parent details).
  • Education details (10th & 12th equivalent from U.S.).
  • U.S. school name and address.
  • Passport or Aadhaar (if available).
  • Scanned photo and signature (as per brochure specifications).
  • Payment method (international card or Indian card/UPI).

Step 4: Pay the Application Fee Online

  • Go to Fee Payment on the official AP EAPCET website.
  • Enter basic details.
  • Pay using:
    • Credit/debit card,
    • Net banking,
    • UPI (if someone in India pays).
  • Save the Payment ID carefully.

Step 5: Fill the Online Application Form

  • Enter Payment ID, mobile number, and date of birth.
  • Fill in:
    • Personal details.
    • Educational details.
    • Communication address (U.S. + India if applicable).
  • Upload:
    • Photo.
    • Signature.
  • Select exam centers in Andhra Pradesh.
  • Submit and save the Application Number.

Step 6: Use the Correction Window (If Needed)

  • Log in with Application Number and date of birth.
  • Edit allowed fields during the correction window.

Step 7: Download the Hall Ticket

  • Visit the official site near the exam date.
  • Enter Application Number and date of birth.
  • Download and print multiple copies of the hall ticket.

Step 8: Travel to India for the Exam

  • Arrive in India 5–7 days before the exam.
  • Carry:
    • Hall ticket.
    • Passport or Aadhaar.
  • Reach the exam center early on exam day.

Step 9: After the Exam

  • Check results online on the official website.
  • Download the Rank Card.
  • Register for counseling when announced.
  • Upload certificates and complete verification as required.

Summary Checklist

  • Check dates.
  • Gather documents.
  • Pay fee.
  • Submit application.
  • Download hall ticket.
  • Travel to India.
  • Write exam.
  • Attend counseling.
