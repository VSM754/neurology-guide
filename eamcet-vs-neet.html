EAMCET vs NEET

A complete comparison to help students choose the right exam.

Overview

NEET is required for MBBS and BDS programs (medical path), while EAMCET is used for engineering, pharmacy, and agriculture admissions in AP/Telangana.

What Each Exam Is For

NEET

  • Required for MBBS & BDS
  • National-level exam
  • Used across India and abroad

EAMCET (EAPCET)

  • State-level exam (AP & Telangana)
  • For Engineering, Pharmacy, Agriculture
  • Not valid for MBBS

Major Differences

Category NEET EAMCET
Purpose Medical (MBBS, BDS) Engineering, Pharmacy
Level National State
Subjects Physics, Chemistry, Biology PCM or Biology
Difficulty High Moderate
Required for MBBS? Yes No
Exam Mode Offline Online

Which Exam for Neurology?

NEET is mandatory to become a neurologist because MBBS is required.

Can You Take Both?

  • Yes — NEET for medicine
  • EAMCET as backup options

Summary

  • NEET = medical careers
  • EAMCET = engineering & pharmacy
  • Both can be taken together
